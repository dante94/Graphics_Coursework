Instructions:
W = Move camera forward
S = Move camera backward
A = Move camera to the left
D = Move camera to the Right
Shift = Move camera up
Space = Move camera down
Right Arrow(->) = Go to the next scene
Left Arrow(<-) = Go to the previous scene
PAUSE = Cycle through all scenes. Press it again to stop
Hold X = Constantly move the camera forward(Press it when you enter scene 3)
